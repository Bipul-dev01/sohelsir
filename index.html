Our Courses

We provide academic courses for students of Classes 5 to 12 in English, Math, and Science.

Class 5 – English

Focus on grammar, reading, and writing skills with fun activities and worksheets.

Class 5 – Math

Build a strong foundation with problem-solving techniques and visual examples.

Class 5 – Science

Explore basic science concepts through experiments and simple explanations.

Classes 6–8 – English

Develop reading comprehension, vocabulary, and writing confidence.

Classes 6–8 – Math

Learn arithmetic, geometry, and algebra through real-life problem examples.

Classes 6–8 – Science

Understand scientific principles with practical experiments and visual learning.

Classes 9–10 – English

Prepare for SSC exams with grammar practice, writing skills, and test strategy.

Classes 9–10 – Math

Complete SSC syllabus with past paper practice and conceptual guidance.

Classes 9–10 – Science

Physics, Chemistry, and Biology lessons made simple and exam-focused.

Classes 11–12 (HSC) – English, Math & Science

Advanced preparation for board exams with expert guidance and model tests.
